Hand Spinners

Hand spinners are the newset thing to hit the market and thousands are flocking to this new fidget toy. These spinners spin between ones two fingers with the push of another finger. They're made with a Ti light weight bearing centered in the middle of the custom hand crafted spinner resulting in an amount of rotations that must be seen to beleive.

To add to the speed of the spin, located on the outer edges of the spinner are removable slides which when taken off reveal weighted plates that can be added or taken off depending on how fast the user wants their spin to be. Once spinning these spinners create designs and patterns in their circle motion to pleasure even a baby. With its simple yet elegant design spinners are the best way to fidget on the go, in class, or even to fidget around.
